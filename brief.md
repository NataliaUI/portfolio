# Project Brief

## Project Name
Natalia Portfolio Website

## Purpose
Create a polished personal portfolio website that introduces Natalia as a UX designer and researcher, highlights her design philosophy, and makes it easy for visitors to connect with her.

## Project Goals
- Present Natalia as a thoughtful and capable UX professional
- Communicate a modern, confident, and human-centered design voice
- Showcase key strengths such as research, strategy, and product design
- Create a strong first impression with a visually immersive landing page
- Encourage visitors to reach out for collaboration or opportunities

## Target Audience
- Potential clients
- Hiring teams
- Collaborators and professional contacts

## Content Goals
The website should clearly communicate:
- who Natalia is
- what she does
- how she approaches design
- why her work matters
- how to contact her

## Visual Direction
- Dark, premium, and modern aesthetic
- Deep navy and purple gradient background
- Bold headline typography with a refined editorial feel
- Rounded cards, soft shadows, and glass-like surfaces
- Clean spacing and a layered visual hierarchy

## Layout Requirements
- One-page structure with a centered content container
- Hero section for introduction and positioning
- About section for background and philosophy
- Highlights section for expertise areas
- Contact section for outreach

## Interaction Notes
- Smooth scrolling for in-page navigation
- Subtle hover effects for links and buttons
- Soft motion and depth through gradients and shadows
- Responsive behavior for mobile and tablet screens

## Technical Notes
- Built as a static website using HTML and CSS
- Lightweight structure with simple file organization
- Easy to maintain and expand over time
